Beach side

Walking by the beachspiaggia gives always a lot of calm to you, and it allows to regenerate your senses.
The beach of Pellestrina is 12 kilometerschilometri long so you have a lot of time and space to reorder your thoughts while walking.
And of course you meet your cousincugino Niccolò while walking, he is doing some works in his cabincapanna and you spend some time with him.
While discussing with Niccolò you are also observing all the little constructionscostruzioni and decorations that other kids do all along the beach.

Niccoló invites you to go to have lunch at grandma's with him and all the other cousins, and you can either choose to accept the invitation or decline it and eat at home by yourself.
