About me

Hello,

I am Cristian, I am a twenty three-year-old guy from Venice, Italy.

Well, I always say that I am from Venice when I actually am from Pellestrina, but no one ever knows it so I go for the most famous city close to it.

Pellestrina is an island located in the north of Italy and forms part of Venice, it counts three-thousand habitants and it is extremely small: twelve kilometers long and two-hundred meters wide. It only has two main long streets where only one bus-line goes back and forth and it is a piece of land between the Venetian lagoon and the Adriatic Sea.

I studied Tourism and Hospitality during High School, and after that I moved to Australia for almost two years where I worked, travelled and studied.

Right now I am attending the second year of a Web Design and Development course at Edinburgh Napier University in the United Kingdom.

As of now I am living in my small italian island, but will be back in Edinburgh soon.
